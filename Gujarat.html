SOMNATH

The Somnath Temple, located in Gujarat, India, is one of the twelve Jyotirlinga shrines of Shiva. Its origins trace back to ancient times, believed to be first constructed in gold by the Moon God, Chandra, in honor of Lord Shiva. The temple has been destroyed and rebuilt multiple times throughout history, reflecting its resilience and significance in Hindu culture. The current structure, completed in 1951, stands as a symbol of enduring faith and devotion.

Timings

Darshan : 6 am to 10 pm
Aarti : 7 am , 12 noon , 7 pm
Light Show : 8 pm to 9 pm
